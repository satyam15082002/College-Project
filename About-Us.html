About the college

Global Institute of Engineering & Management was established by Global Nature Care Sangathan’s Group of Institutions (GNCSGI), Jabalpur to promote quality education in various Technical, Medical & Management fields. The trust was founded in 1999. The trust prides itself of establishing various educational Institutes since its inception.

Global Institute of Engineering & Management is a self-financed institution affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal and Rani Durgavati Vishwavidyalaya, Jabalpur. It is recognized by AICTE & Government of MP. It is specially designed on 10 acres of land with spacious buildings providing an adequate infra-structural facility setup in beautiful natural surroundings. It is landscaped with enlivened architecture that creates conducive environment for learning. The campus is located at approximately 8 km’s from Jabalpur Railway Station and about 3 km’s from Main Bus Stand, with transportation and conveyance facilities readily available.

At GLOBAL we are committed to create an environment of Academic freedom so that each mind can discover, learn and undergo.
